Emergency Help

If you’re in danger, use these first

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger or in crisis, contact local emergency services or one of the support lines below right away.

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

24/7 confidential support

Free, confidential support available 24/7 for anyone in suicidal crisis or emotional distress.

Call 988 Visit Website

Crisis Text Line

24/7 text-based support

Text with a trained Crisis Counselor from anywhere in the U.S.

Text HOME to 741741 Visit Website

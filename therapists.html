What to Expect

Simple intake

Submit your request, then we review goals, preferences, and format needs.

Thoughtful matching

We propose a therapist whose specialty, culture-fit, and schedule align.

Session length

Typical sessions: 50 minutes; family/couples: 75-90 minutes.

Confidentiality

We respect your privacy; limited sharing only as required by law.

Safety, Ethics, and Privacy

Not emergency care: If you are in danger, call 911; for crisis, call/text 988.

Privacy: We protect your information and only share when you consent or when law requires.

Mandatory reporting: We must report risk of harm to self/others or abuse of minors/vulnerable adults.

FAQ

How do you match me with a therapist?

We consider your goals, specialty needs, format preference, availability, and any language or accessibility requests.

Do you offer virtual sessions?

Yes. Most therapists offer virtual, and some offer hybrid or in-person options.

What if I need a different therapist?

Tell us and we will rematch you. Your comfort and fit are important.

Is this real clinical care?

No. This site is an educational demo / school project. For real care, contact a licensed provider.

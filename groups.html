What is group therapy?

Group therapy brings 6–10 participants together with a licensed facilitator to learn skills, share experiences, and practice coping strategies in a safe, confidential setting.

  • Peer support with guided discussion and skill-building.
  • Each session follows a clear structure: check-in, skill, practice, reflection.
  • Confidential, respectful, and inclusive for all participants.

How group therapy works

1) Brief intake

Share goals, safety needs, and preferred format (virtual or in-person).

2) Match & expectations

We align you with the right group and review norms and confidentiality.

3) Weekly sessions

Structured check-in, skill, practice, and reflection with handouts.

4) Progress check

Optional 1:1 check-ins to adjust goals or switch groups if needed.

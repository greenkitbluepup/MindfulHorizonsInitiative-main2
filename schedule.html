FAQ

How long are sessions?

Individual: about 50 minutes. Group: about 90 minutes. Family: about 75 minutes.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, see accepted plans on the Counseling page. We also offer sliding scale self-pay.

Can I do virtual sessions?

Yes, choose Virtual or Either in the format field.

What if I am under 18?

Parent or guardian contact and consent are required. We will follow up with next steps.

What if I need help sooner?

If urgent, call 988 or 911. For faster scheduling, select "This week" and we will prioritize if possible.
